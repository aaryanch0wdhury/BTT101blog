  [QUESTION]
  I work in an office environment.
  While I'm not worried about serious accidents like in a factory environment,
  I am worried about all of the aches and pains I am experiencing daily when I
  put in a full 8 hour day behind the desk. Should I be worried about this?
  What can I do to alleviate these problems?

  Asked by: KumarPatel123 on 30 July 2004 at 04:20


  ANSWER:

    I don't think it's nothing major to be worried about,
    but these aches could get worse if you don't take measures to fix it.
    I suggest maybe asking for a more ergonomic work environment
    from your employer, and if that's not possible,
    you should maybe take quiet walks around the office
    whenever your aches and pains start to flare up.


    Good luck, and I wish you the best!
